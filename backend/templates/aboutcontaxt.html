About RealVision

RealVision is a research‑driven DeepFake detection platform. Our goal is to empower individuals and organizations to verify digital media authenticity in real time.

Our Mission

DeepFakes pose a serious threat to information integrity. We combine state‑of‑the‑art convolutional neural networks with temporal analysis to identify subtle inconsistencies that the human eye misses.

Technology

  • Custom CNN + Temporal Attention for video forensics
  • Optimized for CPU‑only inference (real‑time)
  • Trained on Celeb‑DF v2 & FaceForensics++ datasets
  • 90 %+ accuracy on unseen social‑media clips

The Team

RealVision started as an academic project at BMS College of Engineering, led by Shreesha Hegde. Our interdisciplinary team includes computer‑vision researchers, ethical‑AI advocates, and UX designers.

Roadmap 2025

  • Mobile SDK for on‑device verification
  • Browser extension for social‑media feeds
  • Open‑sourcing core model weights
  • Partnerships with fact‑checking organizations
